DPDP Act & Rules Timeline
Key legislative and regulatory milestones
Aug 2023
DPDP Act 2023 enacted
Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 receives Presidential assent on August 11, 2023. Notified in the Official Gazette.
Jan 2025
Draft DPDP Rules 2025 released
MeitY publishes draft Digital Personal Data Protection Rules 2025 for public consultation.
Apr 2025
DPDP Rules 2025 notified
Digital Personal Data Protection Rules 2025 gazetted. Rules covering notice, consent, breach notification, and grievance redressal come into effect.
2025–26
Data Protection Board constituted
DPB operationalised by Central Government. Digital complaints and inquiry platform launched for data principals.
Jun 2026
Today — enforcement fully active
Full compliance obligations in force. DPB adjudications underway. Data Fiduciaries subject to penalties for violations.
TBD
Significant Data Fiduciary designations
Central Government to notify list of Significant Data Fiduciaries (Sec 10) with additional obligations: DPO appointment, DPIA, and algorithmic audit.
